Teachers

Are you interested in being a supply teacher? If you are qualified, you can easily sign up and start your journey with Vysus: an interactive and secure way of recruiting supply teachers. We provide supply teachers the visibility they require and deserve, link them with the schools they want and furthermore provide a platform where teachers can communicate with their schools. Everything to maximise your experience and furthermore to ensure a for compensation we are taking one of the lowest commissions in the sector. But VYUSUS does not end at this stage. We aim to provide a unique experience and are therefore are looking for potential improvements and the implementation of additional features so together we can change the way supply teachers are recruited.

School

VYSUS is not just a system for supply teachers: VYSUS offers high-quality staff for schools that meet all the requirements the school has set using a unique algorithm. Schools can easily sign-up and start recruiting their supply teacher by creating a job advert, then responding to a few key question and finally a ranking of potential supply teachers will be generated based on relevant academic and professional experience. But VYUSUS does not end at this stage. We aim to provide a unique experience that will facilitate the recruitment by for example supplying a messaging system for easy interactions between potential recruits and schools. We are looking for potential improvement and the implementation of additional features and together we hope to be able to help.
